Lumpia

Lumpiang Shanghai are Filipino-style spring rolls filled with a mixture of ground pork, shrimp, vegetables, and seasonings. They are rolled in a thin wrapper and deep-fried until golden and crispy. Lumpiang Shanghai is often served with a sweet and sour dipping sauce.

Halo-Halo

Halo-Halo is one of the most famous Filipino desserts, known for its colorful and refreshing layers. It consists of shaved ice, sweetened fruits, jellies, sweet beans, and sometimes leche flan, all topped with evaporated milk and a scoop of ube (purple yam) ice cream. It's a delightful mix of textures and flavors, perfect for hot weather.
